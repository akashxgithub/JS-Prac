//css box model

css box model describes the layout and rendering of elements on a web page.
it contains various properties, including borders, margins, padding, and the content.

-Content: This is the content of the HTML element, such as text, images, or other media

-Padding: Padding is the space between the content and the element's border. 

-Border: The border is a line that surrounds the content and padding of the element.Borders can have various styles (solid, dashed, dotted, etc.), widths, and colors.

-Margin: Margin is the space outside the border of the element. It creates space between the element and other elements in the layout.
